Why We Exist

The I Am Healing Foundation is a non-profit organization that was established by a group of women who are dedicated to helping other women who have been traumatized by sexual assault/abuse, domestic violence, and other related experiences.

With our dedicated legal team, clinical psychologists, licensed counselors, it is the foundation's aim to be able to give intensive care, support, and healing to each victim, as well as conducting group discussions, seminars, and learning activities to help families handle and cope with these situations in the best way.

Dedicated Support

We provide on-call counselors and clinical psychologists for any occasion, as well as weekly support groups and webinars.

02.

Effective Legal Team

Whether or not you want to take legal action is up to you. But with I Am Healing, we can assist in providing legal teams to help your case.

We respect that each person has their own way of coping, and zero judgements come from us. We keep everything confidential, and we give you the chance to choose how you want to heal.

I was 8 when I woke up because I felt something poking me inside my underwear. I sat up and found my bestfriend's brother pretending to be asleep beside me. He wasn't supposed to be there.
I was 14 when a boy I had a crush on kissed me at the back of his van. I was surprised to feel more than 2 pairs of hands touching my body. I didn't know his friends hid behind the seats just so they could feel a girl's body.
I was 19 when a close friend invited me for a dinner she was hosting for a businessman. She left me all alone at a club after dinner with that man. Instead of dropping me home he brought me to a motel where I fought and cried but was helpless.

Over the years I've learned to stop pretending that these things never happened to me. I need to remember it no matter how traumatic it was because it could happen to anyone right now. I wish I could tell my 5 year old self that everything will be over soon. That things will get better. And that scar will help us become a beautiful and strong woman someday."
