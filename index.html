Why We Exist

The I Am Healing Foundation is a non-profit organization that was established by a group of women who are dedicated to helping other women who have been traumatized by sexual assault/abuse, domestic violence, and other related experiences.

With our dedicated legal team, clinical psychologists, and registered counselors, it is the foundation's aim to be able to give intensive care, support, and healing to each victim, as well as conducting group discussions, seminars, learning activities and outreach to help families and communities handle and cope with these situations in the best way.

Every woman has a story of power. We bloom when we are around people and circumstances that motivate us. That keep us safe. Stable. Soaring.

But every woman also has a story of struggle. We know what it feels like to always be looking over our shoulder. To always wonder what danger could be lurking in every other corner -- or even in our very homes. But for every tear we wipe away, we can learn to smile. To survive. To stay hopeful. Together.
